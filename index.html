Welcome to the 5th MAMC Anaesthesia Update! The Department of Anaesthesia, MAMC, is excited to host this event from October 15th to October 19th, 2024. This program has become a key platform for postgraduate students across the country, fostering learning, discussion, and innovation.

This year's update features a diverse range of topics designed to aid postgraduate students preparing for their MD, DNB, and DA exams, as well as practicing anaesthesiologists. Our sessions, led by distinguished faculty, will include live case discussions, didactic sessions, interactive workshops, OSCE, music therapy, and recent advances in anaesthesia.

We believe this course will be an enriching experience for all participants, promoting growth and excellence in anaesthesia. We look forward to your active participation in making MAMC Anaesthesia Update 2024 a memorable and educational event.
